About the Creators

We are a group of innovative backend development students, Our journey began at the SKILLUPIMO digital training program, a prestigious skills development program in Imo State, Nigeria. Through this program, we honed the technical expertise and innovative thinking needed to create smart, efficient systems that solve real-world problems. With guidance from industry experts and a commitment to transforming service experiences, we have built QueueSmart to make your waiting time productive and stress-free. At QueueSmart, we believe in leveraging technology to improve everyday life. By offering users the flexibility to schedule their visits and providing businesses with effective queue management, we create a win-win situation for everyone,dedicated to creating effective and user-friendly queue management solutions. Our goal is to streamline processes and improve user experience through smart technology,Join us in revolutionizing the future of queue management!.
